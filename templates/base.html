What is prakriti?

It is the nature of body in terms of dosha. It doesnot change during the whole life of the humnan being. It's responsible for the mental and physical characteristics of the being

What are the basic doshas(components) of prakriti?

The three doshas of Prakriti are Vatta, Pitta and Kapha.

How does prakriti helps us?

It helps in determining the reaction of body and mind when confronted by a stimulus.

Why is the knowledge of Prakriti important?

With the knowledge of one's Prakrati, it is possible to apply Ayurvedic fundamentals in maintenance of health as well as prevention and cure of diseases.

Who created Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is believed to have evolved over thousands of years in ancient India, and it does not have a single attributed founder. Instead, Ayurveda is considered a body of knowledge that developed through the contributions of many ancient scholars and practitioners.
